Our Bonsai team



Our story begins with the interest of a group of friends in Bonsai plants. We recognized that all of us had a strong emotion towards a specific plant type. Soon, we realized that many others had the same feeling as us and we decided to form a group. As we had a good knowledge of Bonsai dealers and sellers, we did not take long to flourish our business. It was about finding the right target audience and with the co-operation of all of our team members, the business started getting alot better. Our business is popular nationwide and have links with 45 other plant businesses nationally and internationally. We provide the best plant products and help people get what they have dreamed of.
